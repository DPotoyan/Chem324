Practice Problems

What you will learn

  • A set of practice problems for Exam 2 spanning the model systems and foundations of quantum mechanics.

  • Topics covered include the harmonic oscillator, the particle in a box (1D and 3D), expectation values, degeneracy, Hermitian operators, and superposition states.

  • Work through these to consolidate the postulates and model systems before the exam.
