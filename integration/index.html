Integration

What you will learn

  • Antiderivatives and indefinite integrals. Integration reverses differentiation, and every antiderivative comes with an arbitrary constant CCC.

  • Definite integrals as Riemann sums. The definite integral is the limit of a sum of rectangle areas, giving the net area under a curve.

  • The fundamental theorem of calculus. This theorem ties differentiation and integration together and is the workhorse for evaluating definite integrals.

  • Techniques of integration. Substitution, integration by parts, and trigonometric identities are the tools you will use to evaluate the integrals that appear throughout quantum mechanics.
