Interactive Features¶

  • Magnitude of the Perturbation (a a a):

    • Increasing a a a increases the influence of the perturbing potential, making corrections to the energy levels and wavefunctions more pronounced.

  • Quantum Number (n n n):

    • Higher n n n states exhibit larger deviations due to their higher energy levels and larger overlaps with the perturbing potential.
